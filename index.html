The Butterfly Effect: This effect is the power to cause a hurricane in a place such as China by a butterfly flapping its wings in New Mexico. It may take a very long time, but the connection is real. If the butterfly had not flapped its wings at just the right point in space/time, the hurricane would not have happened. this is a way to express that small changes in the initial conditions lead to drastic changes in the results.

It's up to you if you believe this or not, but most people do beleive that it is real.

A few videogame and movies have been based off this effect
